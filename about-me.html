The Simple Shift

Hey there, I'm <YOUR NAME HERE>, and the simplicity of life has been my ultimate game-changer. Once upon a time, I was a notorious pack rat. My life was cluttered with possessions that I believed brought me joy, yet I couldn't savor any of it amidst the chaos of excess. Things took a nosedive. I was stuck in a job that drained me, in a home that didn't feel like home, and relationships slipping through my fingers.

Turning the Tide

I reached a breaking point and decided to flip the script. I took a drastic step, tossing 90% of my belongings into the bin, selling my place, downsizing to a smaller apartment, and bidding farewell to the job that fueled my discontent.
